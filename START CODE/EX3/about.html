    Information about Adidas:

  • Adidas is a global sportswear brand founded in Germany in 1949 by Adolf “Adi” Dassler. It is well-known for designing and manufacturing shoes, clothing, and accessories for athletes and everyday wear. The brand focuses on combining performance, comfort, and style.

  • Adidas products often include innovative technologies such as Boost cushioning for energy return in shoes, Primeknit fabric for lightweight and flexible clothing, and moisture-wicking materials to keep athletes dry.

  • The brand is popular not just for sports, but also for fashion and lifestyle, collaborating with designers, celebrities, and streetwear brands to create trendy collections. Their logo, the three stripes, is recognized worldwide as a symbol of quality and style.
